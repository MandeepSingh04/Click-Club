Shubhangi Agrawal

Most Influencial Female Photographer

Conferred as one of the seven photographers globally in the article titled ‘Behind the lens of the upcoming generation of female photographers from all over the world under the age of 25’ which was published along with a small interview and some photos clicked by her in Assembly, a magazine by Pakistani activist and Nobel laureate, Malala Yousufzai (2018). The picture clicked by me was also shared on their official Instagram account.

Won the second prize in the overall category and third prize in the architecture category in the annual online photography competition conducted by MNIT Jaipur (2019).

Six of my photographs have been approved by Photovogue, VogueItalia under the fashion and fine art category.

‘Solace’, a moody portrait series was displayed in OnlyChild magazine's first Virtual Reality Exhibition (2020)

‘Sughar’, a talent and event management company interviewed me on the topic of female photographers to encourage young women who want to enter the photography industry and featured it on their Instagram account.

Eatmynews’, a media platform, featured my interview as a fashion photographer.

FO2, India's exclusive online photography store, as a part of their community initiative to celebrate most influential female photographers in the industry, shared my story on their blog and across their various social media handles.
